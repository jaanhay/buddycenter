Where children with special needs meet, play and just be allowed to be themselves

What We Do

At our organization, we provide engaging workshops tailored for children with special needs, offering a safe and nurturing environment where they can thrive. Our programs focus on developing essential skills, fostering independence, and building confidence. We are dedicated to empowering these individuals to become active and valued members of society.
Through personalized attention and a supportive community, we create opportunities for self-expression and growth. Our expert team collaborates with families to ensure each child's unique needs are met, paving the way for a brighter, more inclusive future.

  • Storytelling Sessions:

    Captivating stories that stimulate imagination and encourage listening skills.

  • Music and Dance:

    Energetic music and dance activities that promote physical coordination and self-expression.

  • Art and Craft:

    Creative art projects that foster fine motor skills and artistic expression.

  • Sensory Play:

    Activities designed to engage the senses, promoting sensory integration and relaxation.

  • Therapeutic Activities

    Tailored activities that support emotional and social development through play.

  • Special Events

    Celebrations and themed events that bring joy and a sense of community.

Vocational Training

Young adults who are capable of basic training are given exposure to various activities to develop skill sets in vocations depending on their capabilities, interest and level of skills. It includes:

  • Arts &Craft

  • Block Printing

  • Paper Bags & Envelope Making

  • Cloth bags,pouches

  • Diya Paintings

  • cooking

  • Arts & crafts

Recreational Centre

  • Timings:10.30 am to 12.30 pm
  • A weekend plan is created to assist each child in individual development of Primary Focus - Facilitate Personality Development, Social Skills & provide exposure in areas of interest, talent and build capability

Project Kianna

Project Kianna, initiated by Buddy Center, aims to provide essential therapies to underprivileged children with special needs, offering services that are often inaccessible due to their high cost and the specialized nature of the therapies.
